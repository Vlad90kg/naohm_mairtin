Design a clean, modern Membership page for a GAA club website (Naomh Mairtin GFC), focused on providing clear information and guiding users to an external membership system (Foireann).

Style:
- Primary color: deep blue (#1E3A8A or similar)
- White/light grey content sections
- Subtle gold accents for highlights
- Rounded corners, soft shadows
- Clean typography with strong headings
- Minimal text, easy to scan
- Consistent spacing and grid

---

GLOBAL HEADER:

1. Tier 1 Sponsor Strip (top):

- Minimal height

2. Navigation Bar (sticky):
- Logo left

  Home, About (dropdown), Teams (dropdown), Events, Lotto, Shop, Membership (active), Sponsors, Contact, Child Safety
