Design a clean, modern “Club History” page for a GAA club website (Naomh Mairtin GFC), presenting historical information in a structured, readable, and engaging way.

Style:
- Primary color: deep blue (#1E3A8A or similar)
- White/light grey content sections
- Subtle gold accents for highlights
- Rounded corners, soft shadows
- Clean typography with strong headings
- Avoid long dense text blocks
- Use visual structure (cards, timeline)

---

GLOBAL HEADER:

1. Tier 1 Sponsor Strip (top):
- Horizontal bar with 3–5 sponsor logos
- Clickable logos
- Minimal height

2. Navigation Bar (sticky):
- Logo left
- Menu:
  Home, About (dropdown), Teams (dropdown), Events, Lotto, Shop, Membership, Sponsors, Contact, Child Safety

---

PAGE STRUCTURE:

3. Page Header Section:
- Blue background banner
- Title: “Club History”
- Short subtitle:
  “A look back at the development and achievements of Naomh Mairtin GFC”

---

4. Introduction Section:

- Short overview paragraph:
  - Founding of the club
  - General background

- Keep text concise and readable

---

5. Timeline Section (MAIN FEATURE):

- Display history as a vertical timeline

Each entry includes:
- Year or period
- Short description
- Optional image (if available)

Examples:
- Club founding
- Key milestones
- Major developments

Layout:
- Clean vertical flow
- Alternating or simple stacked layout

---

6. Key Milestones Section:

- Highlight major achievements or important moments

Display as cards:
- Title (e.g., Championship Win)
- Year
- Short description

---

7. Optional Media Section:

- Small image gallery or historic photos
- Keep layout clean and not overcrowded

---

8. Call to Action (Optional):

- Short text:
  “Be part of the club’s future”
- Button: “Membership Info”

---

9. Footer:

- Address + Eircode
- Email
- Navigation links
- Social media icons
- Clean and compact layout

---

FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS:

- Break content into sections (no long text blocks)
- Use timeline to improve readability
- Maintain clean and structured layout
- Do not include unnecessary complexity
- Ensure responsive design (timeline adapts to mobile)
- Keep design consistent with homepage and app style
