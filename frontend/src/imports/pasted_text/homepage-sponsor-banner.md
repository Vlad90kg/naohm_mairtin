Update the homepage design of a GAA club website (Naomh Mairtin GFC) to include a flexible and visually strong sponsor banner system.

IMPORTANT:
- Do NOT keep the current thin text-based sponsor strip
- Replace it with a proper, scalable sponsor banner section
- Maintain existing colors, layout, and branding

---

GOAL

Create a dedicated "Premium Sponsors" banner area that:
- feels like real advertising banners (not text links)
- integrates naturally into the design
- supports multiple sponsors
- is easy to update, replace, or remove

---

PLACEMENT

Add the sponsor banner section in TWO places:

1. Primary Placement (HIGH VISIBILITY)
- Directly BELOW the header/navigation
- ABOVE the hero section

2. Optional Secondary Placement
- Above footer OR between sections (e.g. before CTA)

---

SECTION DESIGN: "Premium Sponsors"

Create a horizontal banner container with the following:

LAYOUT OPTIONS (auto-adaptive):

Option A (multiple sponsors):
- Horizontal scroll or grid (3-5 banners visible)
- Equal spacing
- Centered alignment

Option B (single sponsor):
- One large full-width banner

The layout should adapt depending on number of sponsors.

---

BANNER DESIGN (IMPORTANT)

Each sponsor should appear as a REAL banner, not text:

- Rectangular cards (wide aspect ratio, e.g. 3:1 or 4:1)
- White or light background
- Logo centered and clearly visible
- Optional subtle border or shadow
- Padding inside banner for clean spacing

Optional enhancements:
- slight hover effect (scale or shadow)
- clickable (link to sponsor website)

---

VISUAL STYLE

- Keep club blue and gold theme
- Do NOT use plain text-only sponsor names
- Use logo + brand identity feel
- Ensure banners stand out but don't overpower hero

---

RESPONSIVENESS

Desktop:
- 3-5 banners in a row

Tablet:
- 2-3 banners per row

Mobile:
- horizontal scroll carousel

---

FLEXIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

Design must support:
- adding new sponsors easily
- removing sponsors without breaking layout
- different banner sizes (auto-fit inside container)
- categorisation (e.g. Tier 1 only in this section)

---

EMPTY STATE

If no sponsors:
- hide the section completely
OR
- show placeholder text: "Sponsor space available"

---

LABEL

Optional small title above section:
"Proudly Supported By"

Keep subtle and clean.

---

DO NOT

- do not recreate thin top strip
- do not use plain text sponsor names
- do not overcrowd banners
- do not break visual hierarchy

---

RESULT

A clean, modern sponsor banner system that:
- looks professional
- gives sponsors real visibility
- integrates naturally into the homepage
