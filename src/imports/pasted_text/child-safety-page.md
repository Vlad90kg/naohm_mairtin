Design a clean, modern “Child Safety (Safeguarding)” page for a GAA club website (Naomh Mairtin GFC), based on common GAA safeguarding page structures but with a simplified, user-friendly layout.

Style:
- Primary color: deep blue (#1E3A8A or similar)
- White/light grey content sections
- Subtle gold accents for highlights
- Rounded corners, soft shadows
- Clean typography with strong headings
- Avoid dense text blocks, ensure readability
- Card-based layout where appropriate

---

GLOBAL HEADER:

1. Tier 1 Sponsor Strip (top):
- Horizontal bar with 3–5 sponsor logos
- Clickable logos
- Minimal height

2. Navigation Bar (sticky):
- Logo left
- Menu:
  Home, About (dropdown), Teams (dropdown), Events, Lotto, Shop, Membership, Sponsors, Contact, Child Safety (active)

---

PAGE STRUCTURE:

3. Page Header Section:
- Blue background banner
- Title: “Child Safety”
- Subtitle:
  “Our club is committed to providing a safe and supportive environment for all children”

---

4. Safeguarding Statement Section:

- Card or highlighted block
- Short statement outlining:
  - Commitment to child protection
  - Alignment with GAA guidelines
- Keep text concise and readable

---

5. Key Contacts Section (IMPORTANT):

- Title: “Safeguarding Contacts”

- Display as cards:
  - Role (e.g. Children’s Officer, Designated Liaison Person)
  - Name (placeholder)
  - Email (placeholder)

- Clean layout, easy to scan

---

6. Policies & Documents Section:

- Title: “Policies & Documents”

- Display list or cards with:
  - Document name
  - Download link (PDF placeholder)

Examples:
- Child Safeguarding Statement
- Code of Behaviour (Underage)
- Reporting Procedures

- Keep layout structured and not cluttered

---

7. Reporting Concerns Section:

- Title: “Reporting a Concern”

- Simple step-by-step content:
  1. Contact club safeguarding officer
  2. Follow reporting procedures
  3. Escalate if required

- Short and clear (no long paragraphs)

---

8. Optional Visual / Document Preview:

- Small image or placeholder for safeguarding document
- Positioned beside or below content
- Keep subtle, not dominant

---

9. Footer:

- Address + Eircode
- Email
- Navigation links
- Social media icons
- Clean and compact layout

---

FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS:

- Do not include excessive legal text
- Focus on structure rather than full content
- Keep layout clean, readable, and structured
- Do not include login or user system
- Ensure responsive design (stacked layout on mobile)
- Maintain consistency with overall site design
